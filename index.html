Our Summerhouse cafe is named after the owner's son, Summer, and is situated at the prestigious address of 2F Gloriana Bella Building, South Poblacion, Valencia, Negros Oriental. This cafe holds a special place in the family of Miss Gerabelle C. Dagoy, as it once belonged to their grandmother and served as a gathering spot for generations. Childhood memories were crafted within its walls, and the breathtaking view of the plaza and its surrounding left a lasting impression. We, at Summerhouse Cafe, relish sharing this cherished place with others.

In 2008, Miss Gerabelle C. Dagoy embarked on an entrepeneurial venture and established a vibrant restobar that thrived for five years. Now, she desires to recreate that magic by transforming it into a cafe, offering a more refined and professional experience. At Summerhouse Cafe, you can savor quality coffee, delectable treats, and a welcoming ambiance that fosters a sense of community and shared moments, just like the summerhouse itself.

Come and join us at Summerhouse Cafe, where coffee brings people together and memories are made.
